General Guide Discord Blueprint TikTok & YouTube Shorts

1. General Guide

Before Sharing Your 1st Content

90% of your target audience consists of Shopify dropshippers actively hanging out on platforms like Reddit and Discord, constantly searching for reliable profit-tracking solutions. Your first step is to understand their pain points before pitching any tool.

Content Angles That Convert

Emphasize that spammy links do not work. Affiliates must solve current problems. If they are talking about Facebook Ads scaling, show them how proper net profit tracking prevents them from bleeding money. Focus on value, not just the affiliate link.

Build Your Audience

Focus on building active communities for a consistent $500–$1,000 monthly passive income rather than one-off posts. The real money is in recurring subscriptions, and that requires trust and a loyal audience base.

2. Promoting on Discord

Why Discord Works

Discord is where real, unfiltered e-commerce discussions happen. Profit tracking is a massive blind spot for most beginners and intermediates in these servers.

How to Talk About It

Start with the problem: "I was scaling my ads but realized I was actually losing money." Don't start with the product. Guide the conversation naturally toward the solution.

Two Approaches

  • The Educational Post: Share a mini-guide on how to calculate margins, dropping the tool as a recommendation.
  • The Personal Experience Post: Share a screenshot of your dashboard (even if it's a demo) showing a turnaround from loss to profit.

3. Short-Form Video Strategy

Why Short-Form Works: It surfaces the blind spot (Revenue vs. Real Profit) in 20-40 seconds to a massive algorithmic audience.

01

"Revenue Looks Good. Profit Doesn't."

Start by showing a massive Shopify dashboard with $10k revenue, then reveal the actual net profit is $400. This pattern interrupt hooks dropshippers instantly.

02

"Scaling Doesn't Always Mean More Profit"

Explain the trap of scaling too fast without tracking COGS and shipping. Frame TrueProfit as the 'safety net' before touching the scale button.

03

"Before I Scale, I Check This One Number."

Build curiosity. What is the one number? Net Profit Margin. Show exactly where to find it on the TrueProfit mobile app dashboard.
