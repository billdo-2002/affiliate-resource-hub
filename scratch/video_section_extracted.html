3. Short-Form Video Strategy

Why Short-Form Works: It surfaces the blind spot (Revenue vs. Real Profit) in 20-40 seconds to a massive algorithmic audience.

01

"Revenue Looks Good. Profit Doesn't."

Start by showing a massive Shopify dashboard with $10k revenue, then reveal the actual net profit is $400. This pattern interrupt hooks dropshippers instantly.

02

"Scaling Doesn't Always Mean More Profit"

Explain the trap of scaling too fast without tracking COGS and shipping. Frame TrueProfit as the 'safety net' before touching the scale button.

03

"Before I Scale, I Check This One Number."

Build curiosity. What is the one number? Net Profit Margin. Show exactly where to find it on the TrueProfit mobile app dashboard.
